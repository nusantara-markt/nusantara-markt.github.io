the 76th Indonesian Independence Day in Bremen

By News

Bremen, August 21, 2021—In this event commemorating the 76th Indonesian Independence Day organized by "DIASPORA Indonesia in Bremen e.V." located in the park of the Focke Museum Bremen, Nusantara-markt participated in the event to promote our product: HALAWA cooking seasoning.

In this event we handed out and introduced Halawa products and also demonstrated a demo cooking Indonesian cuisines using Halawa seasoning as it’s main flavoring.

This event is not only a meeting point for Indonesian citizens in Bremen and its surroundings after a long absence during the pandemic, but also a stage for Indonesian diaspora entrepreneurs to sell and promote their products.

The event was also attended by the Indonesian Consulate General in Hamburg, Mr. Ardian Wicaksono.
