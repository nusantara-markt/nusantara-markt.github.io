About Us

Background

As one of the 20 countries with the largest economic growth and the most muslim-populated country in the world, we believe that Indonesia has the potential to penetrate European Market through Halal and Asian products.

We want to empower small and micro business in Indonesia, especially Pesantren-backed business, to actively contribute and promote their products in European Market and become one of the most valuable assets for Indonesian economy.

What to do

To reach that vision, we founded Nusantara UG, the company that will help small and micro businesses in Indonesia to market and distribute their products in Europe and stands as their partner. We also believe that Nusantara UG will be the embodiment for that spirit and vision.

Impact

Together, we believe that we could build a sustainable economic ecosystem for the small and micro businesses in Indonesia. This would impact not only Indonesian market capital and income, but also to build the spirit to innovate and develop Indonesian products.

We believe that this will be a steppingstone to mass introduce Indonesian products in European Market.
